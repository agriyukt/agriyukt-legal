Privacy Policy for AgriYukt

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

AgriYukt is a digital agriculture platform that connects farmers, field helpers (inspectors) and buyers to enable timely, transparent and efficient trading of agricultural produce. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and protect your information when you use the AgriYukt mobile application and related services.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Name, phone number and role (farmer, helper/inspector or buyer)
  • Village, farm location and basic address information
  • Crop details such as crop type, quantity, expected harvest date and price
  • Photos of crops and field conditions uploaded by helpers/inspectors
  • Bank account details for future direct payments (if enabled)

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information and app usage data
  • Approximate location (if permission is granted)
  • Log and activity data to improve system reliability
  • Notification tokens for sending important alerts

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage user accounts
  • To verify farms, crops and field data through assigned helpers
  • To match market demand with crop availability and readiness
  • To provide price, yield and harvest timing insights
  • To enable communication between farmers, helpers and buyers
  • To improve platform performance and system security

4. Who Can Access Your Information

  • Helpers / Inspectors: Can view farmer and crop details for assigned villages
  • Buyers: Can view limited crop and location information needed for procurement
  • AgriYukt team: For system maintenance and support
  • Legal authorities: Only when required by law

5. Data Storage and Infrastructure

  • Data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure (AWS)
  • Access is restricted using authentication and role-based controls
  • Images and field data are stored securely with controlled access

6. Payments and Transactions

Currently, AgriYukt supports prototype and pilot-stage payment simulations. In future releases, secure third-party payment gateways may be used for real transactions. AgriYukt does not store debit or credit card information directly.

7. Data Security

  • Encrypted communication using HTTPS
  • Role-based access for farmers, helpers and buyers
  • Secure cloud infrastructure and controlled storage
  • Regular monitoring to prevent unauthorized access

8. Data Retention

  • Your data is retained only as long as required for platform operations
  • Data may be retained if required for legal or audit purposes
  • Users may request account deletion at any time

9. Your Rights

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Control location and notification permissions from your device

10. Children’s Privacy

AgriYukt is intended for users above 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of AgriYukt after updates means you accept the revised policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@agriyukt.in

13. Applicable Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of India and applicable data protection regulations.

14. Consent

By using the AgriYukt application, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
